Why Automate?

The era of copy & paste with chatbots is over.

Chatbots Talk. Agents Do.

ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude are great at answering questions. But when you need something done — a file renamed, a folder organized, a script executed, a report generated and emailed — you're back to copying, pasting, and doing it yourself.

An AI agent doesn't just talk. It acts. It reads your files, runs commands, schedules tasks, and talks to you on Telegram — all while running on your Mac.

Real Work, Not Demos

Here's what an AI agent actually does that a chatbot can't:

📸Batch Process 500 Photos

Classify images by content, resize for web, rename by date and location, move to organized folders — without opening a single app.

📊Morning Briefing, Automated

Wake up to a Telegram message with your calendar, weather, stock prices, and inbox summary — generated and sent while you slept.

💻Write Code, Then Run It

Don't just generate a script — execute it, check the output, fix errors, and commit to git. A chatbot gives you code. An agent ships it.

📁Tame Your Downloads Folder

Automatically sort files by type, rename with sensible names, archive old files, and keep your Mac organized — on a schedule.

🔍Monitor & Alert

Track price drops, watch for keyword mentions, monitor website changes — and get a Telegram ping the moment something happens.

🛡️Security You Control

Three security modes. Command approval popups. Nothing runs without your OK. Your agent is powerful — but it answers to you.

Built With Its Own Tools

KatClaw was developed entirely using an OpenClaw-powered AI agent. The code, the website, the documentation you're reading right now — all built by an agent running on the same platform you're about to install. That's not a marketing claim. It's the workflow.

Runs 24/7 on Your Mac

Set up scheduled tasks and your agent runs them around the clock — checking for updates, generating reports, monitoring anything you care about. It doesn't sleep, and it doesn't need a browser tab open.

Your Data Stays Yours

Everything runs locally on your Mac. Your files never leave your machine unless you explicitly ask. No cloud storage, no third-party access, no surprises. You can even run it with a local AI model for complete privacy.
