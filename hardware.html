Which Should You Choose?

Go with Mac Mini if: You want a "set and forget" agent running 24/7, or you're serious about AI automation.

Go with VM if: You already have a powerful Mac and want to experiment without dedicated hardware.

Go with MacBook if: You want easy access to tweak settings, check logs, and understand how your agent works.
